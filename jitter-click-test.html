What Is Jitter Clicking?

Tensing forearm muscles to create rapid vibrations: 10–14 CPS. Used in Minecraft PvP. Carries RSI risk — max 10 min sessions.

How to Jitter Click

  1. Index finger on mouse button
  2. Arm flat on surface
  3. Tense forearm firmly
  4. Vibration = rapid clicks
