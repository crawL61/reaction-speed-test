CPS Test Guide: How to Click Faster and Increase Your Score

Published by Reaction Speed Test · 6 min read

Your CPS (clicks per second) score measures raw clicking speed. Average: 6-7 CPS. Competitive players target 8-12 CPS. Elite players exceed 14 CPS. This guide covers everything.

Regular Clicking (6-9 CPS)

Index finger, single-handed. Safest and most sustainable. Keys: elbow flat on desk, firm but relaxed grip, press just past actuation point without bottoming out, warm up 30 seconds first.

Jitter Clicking (10-14 CPS)

Tense forearm muscles to create vibrations. Effective but carries RSI risk. Never exceed 10 minutes per session. Stop immediately if you feel pain.

Butterfly Clicking (15-25 CPS)

Two fingers alternating on one button. High CPS but banned in most competitive servers including Hypixel. Check rules before using competitively.

Hardware That Helps

  • 1000Hz polling rate — standard on gaming mice
  • Optical switches (Razer, SteelSeries) — near-zero debounce
  • Light buttons — less actuation force = faster clicking
