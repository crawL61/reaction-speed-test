How to Play Clicker Tycoon

Clicker Tycoon is a free idle clicker game where you accumulate coins by clicking, then spend them on buildings and upgrades that generate passive income automatically. As your empire grows, you unlock new building types, prestige bonuses, and city themes.

Core Mechanics

  • Click: Each click earns coins based on your current click power
  • Buildings: Purchase 12 building types that earn coins per second automatically
  • Upgrades: Multiply click power and building efficiency
  • Prestige: Reset progress for permanent multiplier bonuses and new visual themes
  • Research Tree: Unlock special abilities and efficiency bonuses

Features

  • 🏙️ Living city canvas: Watch your city grow with 12 building types and animated construction cranes
  • 🌤️ Weather events: Random weather affects coin production rates
  • ⚡ Click frenzy: Timed bonus events that multiply click value
  • 🌟 Golden coins: Rare spawning coins worth massive bonuses
  • 📋 Quest system: Complete objectives for rewards
  • 🔬 Research tree: 20+ unlockable upgrades
  • 💾 Auto-save: Progress is saved automatically to your browser
  • 🎵 Sound effects: Toggleable audio feedback
