What Is Clicker Tycoon?

Clicker Tycoon is a free idle incremental game built into your browser. Click the large coin button to earn coins, then spend coins on upgrades that generate income automatically while you are away. The core gameplay loop — click, earn, buy, earn passively, prestige, multiply — is designed to be equally rewarding in 5-minute sessions or left running for hours.

All progress is automatically saved to your browser's localStorage. No account needed, no download, and your city building progress persists between sessions.

Prestige System

Once you earn 100,000 total coins, the Prestige button activates. Prestiging resets coins and upgrades but grants a permanent ×2 income multiplier on everything. Subsequent prestiges require 10× more total earnings (1M, 10M, 100M...) but each multiplier stacks — after 10 prestiges your total multiplier reaches ×1,024. The research tree (unlocked after first prestige) provides additional permanent bonuses purchased with RP (Research Points).
