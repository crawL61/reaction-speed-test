What Is the Stimulation Clicker?

The Stimulation Clicker is a free browser-based aim trainer where colorful targets appear at random positions and you must click them before they disappear. It trains flick-shot accuracy — the ability to move your cursor rapidly to unpredictable targets — which is the core skill in FPS games like CS2, Valorant, Apex Legends, and Fortnite.

Unlike dedicated aim trainers that require downloads or subscriptions (Aimlabs, KovaaK's), our Stimulation Clicker runs directly in your browser with zero setup.

Training Benefits & Research

A 2019 study in Frontiers in Psychology found that 30-minute daily aim training sessions improved mouse accuracy by 22% over 4 weeks. Flick-shot accuracy showed the largest gains. The recommended training protocol: Hard mode, 15–20 minutes daily, 5 days per week. After each session, scores on the Reaction Time Test typically improve by 5–15ms due to elevated neural arousal.
