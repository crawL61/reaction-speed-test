About the Dinosaur Game

The Chrome Dinosaur Game (also called the T-Rex Runner or "No Internet Game") is a side-scrolling endless runner built into Google Chrome, originally released in 2014. It activates when you attempt to visit a webpage without an internet connection. Our version lets you play it anytime, online, in your browser — no internet disconnection required.

The game features a T-Rex dinosaur running through a desert landscape. Cacti of various sizes and pterodactyls at different heights appear as obstacles. You jump over cacti (Space or Up Arrow) and duck under pterodactyls (Down Arrow). The game speeds up every 100 points, requiring progressively faster reactions.
